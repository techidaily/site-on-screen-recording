Automatic Capture â€“ You can capture the screen automatically and repeatedly after a certain interval. Once you set the timer, ShareX will take screenshots repeatedly after the set seconds and save automatically for you to check when you are available.

After Capture Tasks â€“ You can copy a screenshot to the clipboard so that you can paste anywhere you want. You can upload on image browsing platforms such as Imgur, Flickr, and Google Photos. ShareX screen recorder is available for Windows users. You have to download ShareX from its official website and it is completely free to download, install, and use.
